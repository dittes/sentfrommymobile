This site exists to remind you of the differences in communication with mobile devices. Here are a couple of things to keep in mind:

Form & style

If you wonder why I didn't use a proper form of a formal email to message you - this is because it is super hard to type on these little devices. But also to just focus on the important points.

PS: I love to keep my mails short any way - so feel free to also use quick replies.

Short message

Due to the fact that this message was sent from a small device without real keyboard, I kept this message rather short. I would have loved to tidy everything up more, but its just not as easy on a mobile.

Typos

Mobile device keyboards are not always as good as they should be. So if you find any typos, wrong grammar or such things, please forgive me for not writing a proper text.
